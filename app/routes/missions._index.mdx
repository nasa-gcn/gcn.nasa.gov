Notes:

- All GCN Classic Notice types are available via [email or Kafka](docs/notices/consuming) in all of the 3 formats (text, binary, VOEvent XML).

- In Development indicates either onboarding missions or active missions that are in the process of developing GCN Kafka Notices while maintaining GCN Classic Notices.
