# Subscribing to Circulars

## To receive Circulars via email:

<ProcessList>
  <ProcessListItem>
    <ProcessListHeading type="h3">
      [Sign in / Sign up](/login)
    </ProcessListHeading>
    Create or sign into your GCN account. If you were previously signed up to receive
    GCN Circulars via the legacy GCN Classic circulars service, your preferences
    were automatically transfered. Once you make an account here with the same email
    address, you will be able to modify your access.
  </ProcessListItem>
  <ProcessListItem>
    <ProcessListHeading type="h3">
      [Email Notifications](/user/email)
    </ProcessListHeading>
    Once signed in, click on your email address and select "Email Notifications"
    from the pull down menu.
  </ProcessListItem>
  <ProcessListItem>
    <ProcessListHeading type="h3">Modify Subscription</ProcessListHeading>
    Toggle "On/Off" button to subscribe or unsubscribe.
  </ProcessListItem>
</ProcessList>
