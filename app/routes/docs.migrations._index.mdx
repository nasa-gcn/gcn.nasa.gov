# GCN Classic Migrations

Since the launch of the modernized GCN in 2022, the GCN Team has been gradually migrating interfaces and services from [GCN Classic](https://gcn.gsfc.nasa.gov) to the [New GCN](/).

## GCN Circulars Archive

The GCN Classic [Circulars archive](migrations/circulars-archive), processing, and distribution service was migrated to the new GCN on April 17, 2023. All [legacy user](faq#how-do-i-sign-in-as-a-legacy-gcn-classic-circulars-user) subscriptions and submission privileges were transferred.

## Notices

All legacy GCN Classic Notice types and formats are available via self service subscription [Kafka and email](notices/consuming). Legacy socket and email distribution of Notices continues through GCN Classic, but users are encouraged to move to the new system. To unsubscribe from your GCN Classic legacy subscriptions, please [contact us](/contact).

## VOEvent Transport Protocol

[VOEvent Transport Protocol distribution](migrations/vtp) of GCN Classic Notices was retired on April 6, 2026. VOEvent format Notices remain available via [Kafka and email](docs/notices/consuming).
